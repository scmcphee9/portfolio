About Me

I am from the east coast: Massachusetts, Maine, and Pennsylvannia. I moved out to Colorado a little over a year ago to a small ski town in Steamboat Springs.

I have been married for 5 years to my ever supporting wife Sarah, and together we have a two year old son William. We also have a German Shephard and two cats.

My interests are in the outdoors, this includes hiking, biking, skiing, scuba diving, etc. Photography is something I have recently gained a passion for. In particular night photography and macro. If you want to see some of my work check out my portfolio.
